Background

Hello There! My name is Zormeme Kinanee. I am a 23 year old currently living in Denver, Colorado. I was born in Nigeria and moved to the US at the age of 5. One of my life goals is traveling the world and going skydiving.

Computer Science

I initially became interested in Computer Science and programming my second year of high school. I'd enrolled in a Web Design course where I was introduced to HTML and CSS. Our final project for the class required we recreate the front-end of a small business's website and since then I've been hooked.

I studied Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder with a minor in Technology Arts and Media (TAM). I focused my studies on web development and design, taking courses like Software Development, Web Design, Database Systems, Image, and Text to cater to the field. I'm ambitious, organized, and dedicated and looking to work with positive people.
