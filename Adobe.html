Below are designs I've done for a Text class taken at the Unveristy of Colorado Boulder. The designs were done using either Photoshop, Illustrator, or InDesign, or a combination of the editors. Enjoy!

Colorado Symphony Abstract Poster

In 2014, The Colorado Symphony hosted a concert at Red Rocks with the theme "Classically Cannabis: The High Note Series." This poster is an abstract black and white and simplistic visual of the theme of event.

Colorado Symphony Poster Mailer

This is another poster made to advertise The Colorado Symphony. This uses abstract shapes and color theory to fit the concerts classical music theme.

What's Cooking Cookbook

In this PDF are cookbooks which I formated using InDesign. The pdf contains three different recipes. The focus with this design was styling, making sure the information was provided in a way that would be appealing to readers

Knowing Your Type

This lab was designed to assess the design and appearance of a typeface. The pdf contains identifiers can can be used to quickly recognize a typeface.
