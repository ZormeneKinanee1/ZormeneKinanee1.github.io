The second step of the project was adding colors, materials, and textures to these objects to give them a realistic feel. Selected materials such as metal and wood were made available on the software and textures were imported. Bumb maps were created using Photoshop, allowing textures to appear sharp on objects. Photorealism was a goal for the project.

The final step was rendering 2160 frames of my created 3D interior model, which took about 32 hours. The rendered frames were imported into Adobe Premiere and when played in sequence created a short video. The animation aspect of the video was the moving of the cameras in the 3D plane through my scene.
